What we do...

Lead Training

Mondays at 6pm

Jesus Green, Cambridge, CB5 8AL


In this session we will concentrate on lead training. This will involve teaching your puppy to walk beside you with a slack lead. Also, we will learn how to prevent unwanted pulling and jumping when it comes to approaching other dogs. This will help create a much more relaxed daily walk for you and make walks enjoyable rather than a chore.

Socializing

Wednesdays at 6pm

Jesus Green, Cambridge, CB5 8AL


In this session we will introduce your puppy to socializing with other puppies of all different sizes and breeds. Which in time will get your puppy used to being around other dogs and prevent the “my dog doesn’t like other dogs” excuse.

Clicker Training

Sunday at 10am

Jesus Green, Cambridge, CB5 8AL


In this session we will help you to understand how to use a clicker to train your puppy. These little tools are so efficient for puppy training. They help create a positive reaction when you want your puppy's attention. This combined with food is a big part of puppy training and it’s worth utilizing.
