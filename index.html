Why puppy training is so important?

Anxiety

Dog anxiety has become a huge factor in this day and age. People working from home and being with your dog is great... Unitil, you go out and your dog suddenly starts losing its mind.

To counter this a good habit would be to have a crate, which will allow you to create that alone feeling for your puppy while you are at home. Then, when you leave the house over time your puppy will become used to being alone and it will be a much smalled shock to the system.
